Introduction back to ToC

Vocabulary for the representation of data on noise pollution that includes the measurement stations and their observations. This vocabulary has been developed in the context of the "Open, collaborative and interoperable government platform" project, in which the city councils of Madrid, Zaragoza, Santiago de Compostela and A Coruña participate. The vocabulary reuses terms from the [geosparql](http://www.opengis.net/doc/IS/geosparql/1.0), [schema.org](http://schema.org/), [Dublin Core](http://purl.org/dc/terms/) and [Dirección Postal](http://vocab.linkeddata.es/datosabiertos/def/urbanismo-infraestructuras/direccion-postal/) which include concepts and relationships that are used for similar purposes. Additionally, it refers to [sosa](http://www.w3.org/ns/sosa/) ontology, in order to observe a noise pollution measurement station, both for the measurement of the noise level and for the measurement of the vibration level.
